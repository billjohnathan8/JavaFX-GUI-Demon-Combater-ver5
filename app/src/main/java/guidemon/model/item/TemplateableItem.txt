Function: 

When an Item is considered Templatable - use Emergent Systems to Procedurally and Promptly Generate new corresponding Items from the Templatable Item and valid material items. 

E.g: 
Material Items: 
-Iron
-Gold
-Emerald


During Item Creation: 
Templatable Item: Battle Pan
Valid Materials: Iron, Gold

Corresponding Items: Iron Battle Pan, Gold Battle Pan

Prompts the creation of these items as well and assignment of their default values.
